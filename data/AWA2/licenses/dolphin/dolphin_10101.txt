Common Bottlenose Dolphin - Madeira, Portugal


+---------------+
|  DESCRIPTION  |
+---------------+
17 September 2013 Common Bottlenose Dolphin / Tuimelaar (Tursiops truncatus) - Madeira (Atlantic Ocean), Portugal

Whale, dolphin and bird watching excursion by boat with Madeira Wind Birds.
Spotted several Common Bottlenose Dolphins (NL: Tuimelaar) (Tursiops truncatus).
