Southern White Rhinoceros (Ceratotherium simum) taking a mud bath


+---------------+
|  DESCRIPTION  |
+---------------+
The white rhinoceros or square-lipped rhinoceros (Ceratotherium simum) is one of the five species of rhinoceros that still exist. It has a wide mouth used for grazing and is the most social of all rhino species. The southern white rhino has an estimated 17,460 wild-living animals at the end of 2007. 

Photographed at the San Diego Zoo Safari Park in Escondido, CA
