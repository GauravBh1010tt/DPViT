Military Police Working Dog demonstration for Humphreys Elementary School First Grade students Oct. 7, 2015


+---------------+
|  DESCRIPTION  |
+---------------+
Humphreys Elementary School first grade students got a treat today when the Military Police Dog Handlers from the 503rd MP Working Dog Detachment brought a few of their dogs to the school and demonstrated some of their  capabilities.  

After the demonstration the kids had the chance to pet a couple of the dogs and talk with the MPs.

U.S. Army photos by Bob McElroy
