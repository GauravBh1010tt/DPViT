Humpback Whale (Megaptera novaeangliae)


+---------------+
|  DESCRIPTION  |
+---------------+
A photo of a humpback whale rostrum with tubercles.  The tubercles (or knobs) are essentially a hair follicle, with one hair each.  The tubercle is filled with sensitive nerves and we have no idea what their function is...
