Brydes Whale


+---------------+
|  DESCRIPTION  |
+---------------+
These mammals are moderately sized rorquals while the average length spans from 45 – 50 feet. The Bryde’s whale is also a baleen whale and belongs to the same group as blue whales and humpback whales. A significant feature is that this species have twin blowholes, no teeth and 2 two rows of baleen plates.
