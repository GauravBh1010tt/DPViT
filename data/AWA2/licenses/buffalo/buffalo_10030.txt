A surrogate mother bison standing guard over her new baby


+---------------+
|  DESCRIPTION  |
+---------------+
A surrogate mother bison stands guard over her calf. The young bison is part of the Laramie Foothills Bison Conservation Herd, which is a group of genetically-pure and disease-free bison established by Colorado State University, U.S. Department of Agriculture (USDA) Animal and Plant Health Inspection Service (APHIS) Veterinary Services, the City of Fort Collins and Larimer County, Colorado. Photo by John Eisele, Colorado State University.
