Czechoslovakian Wolf Dogs / Tschechoslowakischer Wolfshund


+---------------+
|  DESCRIPTION  |
+---------------+
The Czechoslovakian Wolfdog is a relatively new breed of dog from Eastern Europe. It is a cross between a German Shepherd and a Eurasian wolf. It looks mostly like a wolf but behaves like a dog. Until  2008 it was classified a 'Dangerous Wild Animal' in the UK.
