獅子 LEO


+---------------+
|  DESCRIPTION  |
+---------------+
自從買了200mm小砲之後，事隔近一年終於抽空去動物園拍照，那天無奈天陰氣沉，拍啥照片都不像樣，這隻獅子例外......獅子會故意對著遊客噴尿，所以獅子沒辦法用圍欄關著，要用強化玻璃，這照片是隔著強化玻璃，用橡皮製的遮光罩拍的；附帶一提，獅子的活動力強，很難拍......

Since  I bought a EF 55-200 lens, l'd want to go to city zoo again, some day, I met a male lion and took this photos, lions are vigorous and not easy to gat a chance to shoot, and, male lions like to attack visitors by urine, so they can not be separate by only iron enclosure, I took this photo out of the tempered glass, using soft lens hood.
