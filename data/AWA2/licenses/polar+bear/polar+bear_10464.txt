polar bear brother and sister - San Diego Zoo


+---------------+
|  DESCRIPTION  |
+---------------+
Polar bear twins at the San Diego Zoo in San Diego, California.

Kalluk (&quot;Thunder&quot;), the brother, is at the left.  Tatqiq (&quot;Moon&quot;) is at left.  The polar bear (Ursus maritimus) male weighs about 775 to 1,500 pounds and is 8 to 10 feet in length, while a female is half that size.

Polar bears are endangered in the wild due to global warming.
