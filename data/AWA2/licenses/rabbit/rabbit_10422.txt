European Hare washing closeup.
The hare walked right up to me, stopped, and started washing as if I wasn't even there. After a few minutes, he continued on his way and never noticed me or my 3 dogs!
#
Wassende Europese Haas.
Deze haas kwam recht op me af, stopte en begon zichzelf te wassen. Na enkele minuten ging hij verder zonder mij en mijn 3 honden ooit gezien te hebben.
