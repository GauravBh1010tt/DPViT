Thompson's Gazelle


+---------------+
|  DESCRIPTION  |
+---------------+
Thompson's Gazelles are beautiful, elegant creatures, and are characterized by a thick black stripe running on their side and hind legs and a milky white underbelly. This handsome fellow is looking at something in the grass. Many of these beautiful creatures are eaten by the big cats and pythons all the time. (Oct/ Nov 2010)
