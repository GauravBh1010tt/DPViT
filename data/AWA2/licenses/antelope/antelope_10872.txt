Speke's Gazelle (Gazella spekei)


+---------------+
|  DESCRIPTION  |
+---------------+
Speke's Gazelle (Gazella spekei) is the smallest of the gazelle species. It is confined to the horn of Africa where it inhabits stony brush, grass steppes, and semi deserts.
