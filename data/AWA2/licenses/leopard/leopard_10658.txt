Sri Lankan Leopard


+---------------+
|  DESCRIPTION  |
+---------------+
Sri Lanka has a rich and exotic variety of wildlife. The jungles of Sri Lanka abound in a variety of wildlife, which is surprising for an island of its size in the tropics. The best known national park is the Ruhunu National Park at Yala. There are also two other National Parks at Inginiyagala and Uda Walawe.
